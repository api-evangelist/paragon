A CRM Sync is a pattern where an application pulls all data from the CRM source and listens for subsequent updates. For Sales and GTM SaaS builders it offers simplified CRM data access, unified schemas across providers, and permission control, with use cases spanning AI knowledge bases, data enrichment, and analytics.
