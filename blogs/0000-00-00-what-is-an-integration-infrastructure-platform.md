Developers get burned by existing integration platforms because they need end-to-end integration infrastructure, not one-off abstractions. The article identifies three core challenges (integrations span multiple distinct use cases, authentication cannot be duplicated across platforms, and no single platform covers every scenario) and outlines the fundamentals of a true integration infrastructure platform.
